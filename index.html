I am neko my prefix is n! . I am a simple discord bot that send random anime and real like quotes and jokes.I also have a suggestion system and some interesting minigames also.I hope you will enjoy using me.






Why Neko?



Suggestion System:


  • Let users suggest things and have everyone vote on it
  • Send a message to the user who made the suggestion when a decision is made
  • Keep a separate log channel with suggestions with a mod response
  • Restrict suggestions to a specific channel

  • Fake nitro gift

  • Gunfight


Misc Commands:


  • Channel Nuker that delete s and makes a channel again
  • Anime and Real life quotes
  • Next level jokes to make you laugh
